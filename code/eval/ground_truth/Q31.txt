The GPAI model provider must prepare technical documentation for the AI Office and national authorities, and provide downstream providers with information for integration (Recital 101). As a medical device with AI, the product undergoes third-party conformity assessment under medical device legislation AND the AI Act requirements (Recital 50), with providers having flexibility to integrate compliance processes (Recital 64). The system is high-risk both as a safety component in healthcare and in a listed high-risk domain. The hospital as deployer must use the system per instructions, ensure human oversight by competent staff with adequate AI literacy (Recital 91/93), carry out a fundamental rights impact assessment as a public service provider (Recital 96), and notify the market surveillance authority. The GPAI model provider must cooperate with the AI system provider to enable compliance (Recital 85).
