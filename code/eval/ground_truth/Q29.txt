Recital (140) provides that the Regulation offers a legal basis for providers and prospective providers to use personal data collected for other purposes within the AI regulatory sandbox, but only under specified conditions in accordance with Article 6(4) and Article 9(2)(g) of Regulation 2016/679. All other data controller obligations and data subject rights under GDPR remain fully applicable. Importantly, this Regulation does NOT provide a legal basis for automated individual decision-making under Article 22(2)(b) of GDPR. Providers must ensure appropriate safeguards, cooperate with competent authorities, follow guidance, and act to mitigate identified risks to safety, health, and fundamental rights.
