The prohibitions on manipulative and exploitative AI practices should not affect lawful practices in the context of medical treatment, such as psychological treatment of a mental disease or physical rehabilitation, when carried out in accordance with applicable law and medical standards (e.g., with explicit consent). Additionally, common and legitimate commercial practices, for example in advertising, that comply with applicable law should not in themselves be regarded as harmful manipulative AI-enabled practices. The prohibition targets AI systems that use subliminal components, deceptive techniques, or exploit vulnerabilities to materially distort human behaviour causing significant harm.
