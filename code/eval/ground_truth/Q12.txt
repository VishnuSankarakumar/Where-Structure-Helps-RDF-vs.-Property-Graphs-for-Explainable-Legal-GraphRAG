AI systems specifically developed and put into service for the sole purpose of scientific research and development are excluded from the Regulation's scope. However, any other AI system used for the conduct of research and development activity remains subject to the Regulation's provisions. Product-oriented research, testing, and development activity regarding AI systems also falls outside scope prior to those systems being placed on the market or put into service. Critically, once an AI system falling into scope is placed on the market as a result of such R&D activity, it must comply.
