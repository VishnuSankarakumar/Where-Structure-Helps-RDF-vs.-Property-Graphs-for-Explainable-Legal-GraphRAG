Per Recital (130), under exceptional reasons of public security, protection of life/health, environmental protection, or protection of key assets, market surveillance authorities may authorise placement of AI systems that have not undergone conformity assessment. Law enforcement or civil protection authorities may deploy specific high-risk AI systems without prior market surveillance authorisation, provided authorisation is requested during or after use without undue delay. Per Recital (155), all providers must maintain post-market monitoring systems and report serious incidents (death, serious health damage, critical infrastructure disruption, fundamental rights infringement, serious property/environmental damage). These monitoring obligations apply regardless of how the system was deployed, meaning emergency-deployed systems still require ongoing monitoring and incident reporting.
