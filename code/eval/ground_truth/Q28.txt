Per Recital (158), competent authorities under financial services legislation (defined in Regulation 575/2013 and Directives 2008/48/EC, 2009/138/EC, 2013/36/EU, 2014/17/EU, and 2016/97) should be designated as competent authorities for supervising AI Act implementation for financial institutions. Their powers include ex post market surveillance integrated into existing supervisory procedures. When acting as market surveillance authorities, national authorities in the Single Supervisory Mechanism must report relevant information to the ECB without delay. Some provider obligations (risk management, post-marketing monitoring, documentation) are integrated into existing Directive 2013/36/EU obligations, with limited derogations for quality management and deployer monitoring to avoid overlaps.
