The four conditions are: (1) The AI system performs a narrow procedural task, such as transforming unstructured data into structured data, classifying documents, or detecting duplicates. (2) The AI system improves the result of a previously completed human activity, such as improving language in drafted documents. (3) The AI system detects decision-making patterns or deviations from prior patterns without replacing or influencing human assessment, such as checking grading consistency. (4) The AI system performs only a preparatory task for a later assessment, such as file indexing, searching, text processing, or translation. However, these exceptions do not apply if the AI system involves profiling within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2016/679.
