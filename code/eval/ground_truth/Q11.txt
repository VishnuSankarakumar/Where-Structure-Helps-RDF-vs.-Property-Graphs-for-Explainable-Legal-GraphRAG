An AI system developed for military, defence or national security purposes falls within scope if it is used outside those purposes temporarily or permanently for other purposes, such as civilian or humanitarian purposes, law enforcement or public security purposes. The entity using the system for non-excluded purposes must ensure compliance. Furthermore, AI systems placed on the market for both an excluded purpose (military/defence/national security) and one or more non-excluded purposes (civilian/law enforcement) fall within scope, and providers must ensure compliance.
