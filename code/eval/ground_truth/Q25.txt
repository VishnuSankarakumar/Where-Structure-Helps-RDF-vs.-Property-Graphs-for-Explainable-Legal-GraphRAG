Per Recital (111), the cumulative amount of computation measured in floating point operations is an initial threshold; if met, the model is presumed to have systemic risk. This includes computation from pre-training, synthetic data generation, and fine-tuning. Per Recital (112), providers must notify the AI Office at the latest two weeks after meeting the threshold or learning they will meet it, and may demonstrate the model exceptionally does not present systemic risks. Per Recital (114), models confirmed as having systemic risks face additional obligations beyond standard GPAI requirements: model evaluations including adversarial testing prior to market placement, continuous assessment and mitigation of systemic risks through risk-management policies, post-market monitoring, and adequate cybersecurity protection.
