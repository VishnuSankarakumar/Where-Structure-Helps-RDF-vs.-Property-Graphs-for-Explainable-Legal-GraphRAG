Per Recital (33), use is limited to exhaustively listed situations: search for certain crime victims/missing persons, threats to life/physical safety or terrorist attacks, and localisation of perpetrators/suspects of listed criminal offences punishable by at least 4 years custody. Per Recital (34), use must be limited in time, geographic scope, and personal scope, and requires a fundamental rights impact assessment and database registration. Per Recital (35), each use requires express authorisation by a judicial authority or independent administrative authority (prior, or within 24 hours in urgent cases; rejection means immediate cessation and data deletion). Per Recital (36), the market surveillance authority and national data protection authority must be notified of each use, and they must submit annual reports to the Commission.
