Per Recital (104), open-source GPAI models with publicly available parameters, weights, architecture information, and usage information are exempt from transparency-related requirements. However, this exception does NOT extend to the obligation to produce a training content summary and the obligation to comply with Union copyright law (per Recital 104, connecting to Recital 106 which requires a copyright compliance policy including respect for Article 4(3) of Directive 2019/790 on opt-out rights). If the model presents systemic risk, the open-source nature and transparency are not sufficient reasons to exclude compliance with systemic risk obligations under the Regulation.
