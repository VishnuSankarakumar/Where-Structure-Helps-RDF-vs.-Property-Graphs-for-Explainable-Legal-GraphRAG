Per Recital (84), a third party becomes a provider if it: (1) puts its name or trademark on a high-risk AI system already placed on the market, (2) makes a substantial modification to a high-risk AI system that remains high-risk, or (3) modifies the intended purpose of a non-high-risk system so it becomes high-risk. Per Recital (128), a substantial modification includes changes to the operating system or software architecture, or changes in intended purpose, requiring new conformity assessment. However, changes from pre-determined continuous learning after market placement do not constitute substantial modification if pre-assessed at conformity assessment.
