Per Recital (50), AI systems that are safety components of products or are themselves products falling within scope of listed harmonisation legislation are classified as high-risk under this Regulation if the product undergoes conformity assessment with a third-party body. Per Recital (51), classification as high-risk under this Regulation does not necessarily mean the product itself is high-risk under the relevant harmonisation legislation. Per Recital (124), for such product-related AI systems, compliance should be assessed as part of the existing conformity assessment without affecting its specific logic, methodology, or general structure. Providers have flexibility in how they integrate this Regulation's requirements with existing compliance processes.
